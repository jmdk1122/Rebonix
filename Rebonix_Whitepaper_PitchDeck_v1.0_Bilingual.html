Rebonix Protocol — Behavioral Insurance Layer for Traders

子品牌：FOMOCare — 加密投资者的情绪对冲计划

01. Executive Summary | 01. 项目概览

Rebonix Protocol is the first on-chain Behavioral Insurance Layer that compensates traders for missed opportunities. Users pay a small premium (USDC) to get a fixed payout (k×F) if the market later proves their plan would have been right. FOMOCare is the social consumer brand that turns ‘regret’ into an engaging, viral experience.

Rebonix 协议是首个面向交易员的链上“行为保险层”，为“错过机会”提供参数化赔付。用户支付小额保费（USDC），若后续行情验证其计划方向正确，则按定额倍数（k×F）赔付。FOMOCare 作为前端消费品牌，将“悔单”转化为可传播、可参与的社交体验。

02. Problem | 02. 市场问题

  • FOMO, panic selling, and regret drive irrational decisions.
  • No ‘missed opportunity’ compensation exists today.
  • Emotional risks are not financialized; products insure positions, not regret.
  • FOMO、恐慌抛售与后悔导致非理性决策；
  • 当前缺乏对“错过机会”的补偿机制；
  • 情绪风险未被金融化：现有产品只承保头寸，不承保“后悔”。

03. Solution | 03. 解决方案

Dual-brand structure: Rebonix (protocol) provides parametric insurance and LP pools; FOMOCare (app/brand) provides a fun, shareable ‘FOMO care’ experience to drive adoption.

双品牌结构：Rebonix（协议层）提供参数化承保与 LP 资金池；FOMOCare（应用/品牌层）通过“FOMO 关怀”产品形态驱动用户增长与传播。

  • Small premium (USDC), fixed payout (k×F), oracle automation (Chainlink/Pyth).
  • No dependency on real trades—only the committed plan and price path.
  • NFT policies, transparent on-chain settlement.
  • 小额保费（USDC）、定额赔付（k×F）、预言机自动执行（Chainlink/Pyth）；
  • 不依赖真实成交，只依赖计划承诺与价格轨迹；
  • NFT 保单、链上透明结算。

04. Core Mechanism | 04. 产品原理

  • Commit plan (asset, side, threshold, Δt) → pay premium F.
  • Protocol pricing ensures F > p(trigger) × G.
  • Oracle checks TWAP/Median; if trigger, payout G = k×F.
  • 提交计划（标的、方向、阈值、窗口 Δt）→ 支付保费 F；
  • 协议定价确保 F > p(trigger) × G；
  • 预言机取 TWAP/中位价，触发则赔付 G = k×F。

05. MVP Products | 05. MVP 产品

  • Plan-but-no-trade: planned direction proves right (e.g., +5% in 24h).
  • Missed-Leverage: planned leverage L would have crossed threshold within Δt.
  • Both are independent of actual trading activity.
  • 未下单险：计划方向被验证正确（如 24 小时内 +5%）；
  • 错过杠杆险：计划的杠杆 L 在 Δt 内达到设定阈值；
  • 均与真实交易无关。

06. Economics | 06. 收益模型

Expected profit: E[Π] = F − p(trigger) × G. Fixed payout (capped) with low trigger probabilities yields attractive margins. Example sharing: LP 60%, Protocol 30%, Channel 10%.

期望收益：E[Π] = F − p(trigger) × G。赔付封顶 + 触发低频 → 利润率可观。分配示例：LP 60%、协议 30%、渠道 10%。

07. Risk Management | 07. 风控设计

  • Cooling-off period (10–15 min) before observation.
  • Dual oracles + TWAP/Median to prevent wicks/spikes.
  • Per-policy payout caps; pool tranching (Senior/Junior).
  • Black-swan pause; dynamic premium pricing.
  • 冷却期（10–15 分钟）后进入观察窗；
  • 双预言机 + TWAP/中位价防插针；
  • 单保单赔付上限；资金池分层（Senior/Junior）；
  • 黑天鹅暂停；动态保费。

08. Architecture | 08. 技术架构

EIP-712 signed plan commits; premium engine; oracle module; settlement; LP pool manager; SDK/API for partners; DAO governance for parameters.

EIP-712 计划签名；保费定价引擎；预言机模块；结算模块；LP 池管理；对外 SDK/API；DAO 治理参数。
